reducers.rs has four unwrap() on card_id fields in attack_card and bot_resolve_attack.
All are preceded by tile lookups that confirm the tile exists and has a card:
- `attacker_tile.card_id.unwrap()` called after `attacker_tile` was obtained from board lookup
